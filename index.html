This is the webpage of the University of Manchester Category Theory group, led by Nicola Gambino. We are a subgroup of the University of Manchester's Logic group. Our work includes the research into the following topics:

  • Categorical Logic
  • 2-dimensional category theory
  • Models of dependent type theory
  • Duality Theorems
